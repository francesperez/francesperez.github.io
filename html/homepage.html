About Me

As a child, I was OBSESSED with Neopets, an online game where users could raise their neopets by feeding them and dressing them up. One interesting feature to the site was being able to code a custom petpage for your own neopet! At eight years old, I was attempting to code a pimped out page for my neopet, 'Hippey'. At the time, I had no idea what coding even was, all I remember was getting frustrated and scouring the web for tips on how to position all the cool banners I wanted to add to my pet's page! As the years passed and I got older, I abandoned my neopet and locked that core memory away.
